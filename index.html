Download
Mac Windows Linux
Story
The SDBX Abyss Laboratory conducted an experiment that mutated Gorani, an endangered animal that resembles the look of deers, in order to reinforce the physical power of it. The experiment was successful, but it resulted in one side effect: mutated Goranies are extremely urged to eat the brains of each other. You play as a mutated Gorani. Not allowed to eat any other type of diet, you must kill other Goranies played by other human players if you want to survive.

Gameplay
Your HP will decrease over time. You can recover your HP by three ways. 1. Attack other player. 2. Kill other player. 3. Stay in a campfire. Your goal is to kill as many players as possible and survive as long as possible.
Controls
A attack
S dash
space or W jump
F defense
↑ ↓ → ← movement
You can attack or dash in multiple directions. Choose the direction you want by pressing arrow keys and, simultaneously, press a key or s key.
↑ + A attack upward
↓ + A attack downward
→ + S dash to the right
← + S dash to the left
↑ + S dash upward
